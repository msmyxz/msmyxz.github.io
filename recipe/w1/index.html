how to make a cup of tea.

we will take a quick look at how to make your fav. cup of tea, in a matter of 5 minutes

ingridents

  • tea, your fav. flavor
  • sugar/honey, whatever you like
  • water
  • maybe lemon or menthe

steps

follow these steps carefully

  1. put some water in boiler, turn it on
  2. wait for 5 minutes, during that go to step 3
  3. put some sugar in your cup, you can add menthe ior lemon if you like them
  4. turn the boiler off
  5. pour boiled water in the cup, move it for a while
  6. add menthe or lemon to the cup
  7. voila! and bon appetit
